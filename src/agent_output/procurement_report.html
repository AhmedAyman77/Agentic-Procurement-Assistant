Procurement Report

Executive Summary

This procurement report provides a comprehensive analysis of wallet products from various e-commerce platforms in Egypt, including Amazon Egypt, Jumia Egypt, and Noon Egypt. The report compares prices, specifications, and recommendations for products such as Tommy Hilfiger, RFID blocking, Gucci, minimalist, and Fossil wallets. Key findings highlight price variations, discounts, and product features to aid in informed procurement decisions.

Introduction

The purpose of this report is to assist in the procurement of wallets by comparing prices and features across different e-commerce platforms in Egypt. The report focuses on popular brands and types of wallets, providing detailed insights to optimize purchasing decisions.

Methodology

The data was collected by searching for specific wallet products on Amazon Egypt, Jumia Egypt, and Noon Egypt. Prices, specifications, and discounts were extracted and analyzed. The findings are presented in tables and charts for clarity and ease of comparison.

Analysis

The analysis reveals significant price variations across platforms and brands. Luxury brands like Gucci have higher price points, while minimalist and RFID blocking wallets offer more affordable options. Discounts range from 10% to 25%, with synthetic and aluminum materials being more budget-friendly.

Recommendations

  • Best Value for Money: RFID Blocking Slim Wallet (Jumia Egypt) - Affordable with RFID protection.
  • Luxury Choice: Gucci GG Supreme Canvas Wallet (Noon Egypt) - Premium design and material.
  • Budget Option: Minimalist Front Pocket Wallet (Amazon Egypt) - Lightweight and affordable.

Conclusion

This report provides a detailed comparison of wallet products across major e-commerce platforms in Egypt. By analyzing prices, discounts, and specifications, it offers valuable insights to guide procurement decisions, ensuring the best value for money.
