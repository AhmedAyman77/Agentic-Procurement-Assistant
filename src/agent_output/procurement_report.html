Procurement Report

Executive Summary

This procurement report provides a comprehensive analysis of wallet products from various brands across different e-commerce platforms in Egypt. The report compares prices, specifications, and discounts to assist in making informed purchasing decisions. Key findings include significant price variations across platforms and brands, with notable discounts available on certain products.

Introduction

This report aims to evaluate and compare wallet products from leading brands such as Tommy Hilfiger, Fossil, Montblanc, Hugo Boss, and Guess. The analysis focuses on product availability, pricing, and specifications across platforms like Amazon.eg, Jumia.com.eg, and Noon.com/egypt-en.

Methodology

The data was collected by searching for specific wallet products on the respective e-commerce platforms. Prices, discounts, and product specifications were extracted and compared. The analysis includes both primary and alternative product options to provide a comprehensive overview.

Analysis

The analysis reveals that Montblanc wallets are the most expensive, reflecting their luxury brand status. Tommy Hilfiger and Hugo Boss wallets offer good discounts, making them attractive options. Fossil wallets provide RFID protection, adding value to their price. Guess wallets are the most affordable, suitable for budget-conscious buyers.
