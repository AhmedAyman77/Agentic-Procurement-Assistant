Executive Summary

This procurement report provides a comprehensive analysis of wallet products from various online retailers in Egypt, including Amazon.eg, Jumia.com.eg, and Noon.com. The report compares prices, specifications, and recommendations for products such as Tommy Hilfiger, Fossil, Montblanc, Bellroy, and Secrid wallets. Key findings highlight significant discounts, material quality, and RFID protection features across different brands.

Introduction

The purpose of this report is to assist in the procurement of high-quality wallets by comparing prices and features from leading online retailers in Egypt. The scope includes men's and women's wallets from premium brands, focusing on material quality, design, and additional features like RFID protection.

Methodology

The data was gathered by searching for specific wallet products on Amazon.eg, Jumia.com.eg, and Noon.com. Prices, specifications, and discounts were extracted and compared. Recommendations were based on material quality, discount percentage, and customer reviews.
